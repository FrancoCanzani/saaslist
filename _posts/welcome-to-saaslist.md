# Welcome to SaasList

Welcome to **SaasList**, your go-to platform for discovering and sharing amazing SaaS products!

## What is SaasList?

SaasList is a modern platform inspired by Product Hunt, designed specifically for the SaaS community. Whether you're a founder launching your latest product or a developer looking for the next great tool, SaasList is here to help.

## Features

- **Product Discovery**: Browse through hundreds of curated SaaS products
- **Community Reviews**: Read and write reviews from real users
- **Upvote System**: Support products you love
- **Categories & Tags**: Find products by category or specific tags
- **Leaderboard**: See which products are trending

## Getting Started

1. **Browse Products**: Start by exploring our [browse page](/browse)
2. **Create an Account**: Sign up to upvote, review, and submit products
3. **Launch Your Product**: Share your SaaS with the community

## Join the Community

We're building a vibrant community of SaaS founders, developers, and enthusiasts. Join us today and be part of the future of SaaS discovery!
